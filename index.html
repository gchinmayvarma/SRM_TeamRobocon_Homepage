ABOUT US

We are a diverse team of undergraduate students, sharing a channelized aim of building ingenious technology in the form of robotic systems which on the termination of ABU Robocon can be developed and owing to its uniqueness, accordingly can be filed as patents, research papers.

We work towards re-imagining robotics to innovate and cultivate science beyond what we've seen so far.

We have hands-on experience in a multitude of electromechanical apparatus and equipment and are always in pursuit of creative thinkers and designers, with a view to expanding and refining the troupe.

MISSION

To engage in research work to develop advanced robotic systems.

Reimagine the knowledge from robotics into producing a solution for the growing society, for a better tomorrow.

VISION

Emerge as a renowned robotics systems lab centred at SRM IST, INDIA by inculcating a collaborative work culture.
